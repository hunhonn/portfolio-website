Open Malaysian Rail Information

Personal Project

July 2024 - Present

A passion project that involves collecting and curating online data on Malaysian Railways such as RapidKL and KTM, making it publicly accessible through repositories and APIs that I manage. I aim to support transparency, research, and innovation in the rail industry by streamlining access to this information. This project also encompasses a website that monitors rail disruptions across the Malaysian rapid transit system.
